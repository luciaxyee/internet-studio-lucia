The Pokeballers

This is the Pokeballers, where Pokemon compete in a game of dodgeball! There's Ditto who is the master transformer, Mimikyu who can take more than one hit, Ninetales who can freeze any ball, Turtwig who can grow plants to block balls, and Raquaza who can move super fast and catch balls from a far distance. This team is full of specialized skills and are filled with individulas who are all different in shape, size, and ability!! These pokemon love working together and having fun combining their ablities in new and different ways. While these players are competative, they also just love learning new ways to play the game.
