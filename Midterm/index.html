Truffula Forest

Come join us for a time in the natural forest of cotton-candy like trees! The forest is now regrown and here for you and you loved ones to enjoy, or to take a trip on your own. Next to the forest is the world famous artificial city called Thneedville where you can visit the museum of oxegyn where it had to be purchsed in the history of the town because of the previous state of no trees.

About Truffula trees

  • They grow in colors of orange, yellow, pink, and sometimes purple.
  • The top of the tree is like cotton candy or like soft clouds.
  • The texture is softer than silk.
  • The sent is like butterfuly milk.
  • The seeds take 10 months to germinate, 10 years to become a sapling, and another 10 to become a fully grown tree.
  • The animals that live near the trees find it a place to rest and play.
